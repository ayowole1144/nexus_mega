Who We Are

Nexus Mega Logistics is a leading provider of construction equipment leasing services, specializing in the provision of dump trucks and other heavy machinery for a wide range of projects across Lagos, Nigeria.

Founded in 2024, our mission is to support the rapid development of infrastructure in our city by offering reliable, efficient, and cost-effective logistics solutions to our clients. We pride ourselves on our commitment to safety, timeliness, and customer satisfaction.
